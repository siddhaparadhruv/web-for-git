About Us

Govardhan Institute is a leading computer training institute in Surat, Gujarat, India. It offers a variety of courses in programming languages, web development, software development, and ethical hacking. The institute is known for its experienced and qualified faculty, its state-of-the-art infrastructure, and its focus on practical training.

The institute also offers a variety of short-term courses and workshops on specific topics, such as SQL, Git, and JavaScript. Govardhan Institute has a team of experienced and qualified faculty who are passionate about teaching. The faculty members have extensive experience in the IT industry and are well-versed in the latest technologies. The institute has a state-of-the-art infrastructure with well-equipped classrooms and laboratories. The students have access to the latest software and hardware, which helps them to learn the latest technologies and skills. Govardhan Institute focuses on providing practical training to its students. The institute has tie-ups with leading IT companies, which provides students with opportunities to gain hands-on experience in the real world. Govardhan Institute has a strong track record of success. Many of its alumni are now working in leading IT companies around the world. The institute has also been ranked among the top computer training institutes in Surat.
